A latent space of Rick Rubin songs

Rick Rubin can't play an instrument or read music. Yet he's produced Slayer, Johnny Cash, the Beastie Boys, Adele, Kanye, and System of a Down.

Here is every kind of music he's made on one map. AI listened to each track, and you choose what the directions mean — more aggressive, more acoustic, more sacred. Every song slides to where it belongs, by how it actually sounds.

That map is a latent space. When you prompt an AI, you aren't asking it to copy a song — you're asking it to move in a direction on a map like this one.
